Installation

1. Always place humidifier on a firm, level surface. (A water proof mat or pad is recommended)

2. Keep the cord out of heavy traffic areas, this includes under rugs.

3. Do not place the humidifier under overhanging surfaces to prevent moisture damage.

4. Place the humidifier away from children or anywhere children can access easily.

5. Place the humidifier on an inside wall at least 4 inches (10 cm) away from the wall for best results.

6. Point the humidifier's moisture outlet away from the wall.

7. Always keep the rooms open to stop humidity build up.

Safety

When using electrical appliances, basic safety precautions should always be followed to reduce the risk of fire, electric shock, and injury to persons, including the following:

1. Keep the cord out of heavy traffic areas, this includes under rugs.

2. DO NOT place humidifier near heat sources such as: stoves, radiators, and heaters.

3. DO NOT attempt to refill humidifier without first unplugging the unit. Make sure to pull the plug and not the cord.

4. Keep the moisture outlet clear from debris.

5. DO NOT tamper with the unit while operating. Only remove water tank while the unit is unplugged.

6. NEVER place it on a rug, carpet, or on a finished floor that may be damaged by exposure to water or moisture.

Maintenance

1. This humidifier requires daily and weekly maintenance to operate appropriately. Refer to daily and weekly cleaning procedures.

2. Use only cleaners and additives recommended by the manufacturer.

3. NEVER use detergents, gasoline, glass cleaner, furniture polish, paint thinner, or other household solvents to clean any part of the humidifier.
